Journey that Shaped
Who I am

Singapre. Shanghai. Korea. Before coming to college, I attended six different schools in three different countries while swerving between four different national curriculums.
With my diverse educational background, I took advantage of every moment that each opportunity provided through opening up to unique student cultures and challenges.
Such a dynamic experience across many countries has shaped me as an individual who is not afraid to dip my toes in a new setting and see what the next
opportunity has to offer, knowing that I will mold and change to become more aware of the world around me.
