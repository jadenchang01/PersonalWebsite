About Me

Hello, I'm Jaden!

My name is Jaden Chang, or Inhwa for Korean speakers. I am an adaptable learner, active hiker, empathetic friend, and candid worker, who aspires to constantly learn and understand the world around me.

I recognize that this is a very broad and ambiguous statement for those who have yet to meet me. Therefore, in hopes of better describing who I am as a person outside the rigid framework of resumes and CVs, I have developed a website where I walk you through the significant milestones of my journey, share my proudest achievements, and offer a glimpse into my everyday life.

This website has been entirely crafted from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. While I relied on online tutorials and reference websites for grasping the foundations of the language, I thoughtfully designed and implemented every element of the website, including the overall layout, the color scheme, and contents, without the aid of pre-made templates or external prompts.

With the basic idea of this website in mind, I invite you to explore each section of my website, which I hope will reveal a clearer portrait of who I am.
